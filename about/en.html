About this site

This site is made with Jekyll, a static site generator written in Ruby. Jekyll supports a variety of themes, and the one I'm currently using is called Hacker.

I chose this platform because I'm not very good (I suck, to be honest) at designing user interfaces from nothing, be it apps or websites. It's a subjective thing, and I have more of a knack for objective things, like if code works as intended or not. Of course, I want to get better, and I'm always trying to get better.
So, in my never-ending quest in learning new things, I made this site, so I can play around and have fun, while I learn about good design practices.

This isn't just my personal website, it's also my portfolio site. But I don't want it to be yet another boring portfolio site. I want to show who I am and my weird personality. I want to show I like to have fun and to not take everything too seriously, but when it's time to work, I'm very reliable and responsible. But of course I'd say that, right? ;)
